Abstracts & Pitch Decks

We invite medical students, researchers, and innovators to share their groundbreaking work in our dual-format structure.

Day 1: Research Presentations

May 28th is dedicated to high-quality, peer-reviewed scientific inquiry.

  • Presentation: Oral or Poster.

  • Format: Slide deck submissions (PDF).
  • Presentation: Live pitch to expert panelists.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit my abstract or pitch deck?

All submissions must be made through our dedicated conference portal. Click the "Registration & Portal" button on this page to be redirected to the secure submission form.

Can I present both an abstract and a pitch deck?

Yes. If you have a research abstract (for Day 1) and a distinct innovation/entrepreneurial pitch (for Day 2), you may submit both for review.

Who can attend the conference?

The conference is open to UoN students, students from other universities, healthcare professionals, international scholars, and industry partners.
