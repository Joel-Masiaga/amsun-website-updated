The Culmination

About the Dinner

The Association of Medical Students of the University of Nairobi (AMSUN) organizes the Finalists' Dinner annually to mark the culmination of the medical training journey for graduating MBChB students.

The dinner provides a formal and memorable platform for the graduating class to celebrate their academic achievements, share fond memories of their school journey, while symbolizing the transition of these young doctors into the medical profession.

It creates a unique opportunity for graduating doctors to build networks that support their transition into professional practice, connecting with faculty members, healthcare professionals, alumni, and industry partners.

The Purpose
